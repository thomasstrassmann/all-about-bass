Influential players - The Hall of Fame of bass playing

Marcus Miller

A genius who slaps the bass.... Hardly anyone else brings so much rhythm to the strings and makes this look so playfully easy.

Charles Berthoud

A young generation bassist who taps, slaps and composes wonderful tracks like no other. World class!

Victor Wooten

Victor Wooten doesn't play the bass, he makes it sing. This is what it sounds like when you dedicate your whole life to an instrument...

Tiago Andree

The youngest bass player in this list, but also the most technically demanding player. This virtuosity would normally be expected from someone who has been playing for decades. An exceptional talent!
